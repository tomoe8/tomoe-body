How It Works

Gentle. Precise. Lasting.

ゆるませる　整える　戻る

Most therapies ask your body to work harder — stretch further, push more, build strength. Sometimes that is exactly right. But what if the body first needs to let go?

Téate Alignment starts with release. By gently working with the bones and returning them to their natural position, range of motion comes back on its own — without effort, without pain. You feel lighter. And the change tends to stay.

Sessions are quiet and unhurried. You remain fully clothed throughout. No oils, no pressure, no equipment. Each session is guided entirely by what your body needs on the day.

This approach is rooted in Edo-period Japanese movement research — a tradition that understood natural ease and effortless posture long before modern tension became the norm.

"A loose, free body is not built. It is uncovered."

Did you know? This method is studied by Master Minami, whose clients include professional baseball players, elite athletes, and people recovering from serious illness — all with the same core approach: gentle, precise, and deeply effective.

  • Fully clothed — no oils, no equipment needed

Your Practitioner

Tomoe Seki.

せきともえ

Tomoe Seki せきともえ Body Alignment Practitioner · 和整体師

Born and raised in Saitama, Japan. After a master's degree in African Studies at the University of Edinburgh, I spent years working in humanitarian NGOs and sustainability consulting, and later in M&A financial advisory in Hong Kong.

Living across different cultures brought a deeper question: what does it really mean to be Japanese? That question led to years of tea ceremony practice and a deep study of Yamato Shigusa — the traditional Japanese philosophy of how the body moves and holds itself.

When I moved to London in 2022, I discovered Wa Seitai: a form of bodywork built on the Japanese idea of te-ate — healing through careful, present touch. I was drawn immediately to its approach of releasing rather than pushing. A gentle wisdom that felt, from the start, like something I had always known.

I tested it first on myself, then on my children. The results convinced me. I continue to study under Master Minami, whose practice extends from professional athletes to clients managing serious illness. I bring the same care to every person who comes through my door.

2022年の渡英後、「手当て」を核とする「和整体」と出会います。「ゆるませる」という考え方に深く共鳴し、まず自分自身で実感。その後、子どもへの実践を通じて確信を深めました。

Questions & Answers

Things people often ask.

よくあるご質問

Massage works on muscles — releasing tension through pressure and touch. Téate Alignment works with the bones, gently guiding them back to their natural position. When the bones are aligned, the muscles around them relax on their own. The result tends to be longer-lasting, because it addresses the underlying structure rather than the surface tension.

マッサージは筋肉に働きかけますが、Téateは骨に直接アプローチします。骨が本来の位置に戻ると、まわりの筋肉も自然とゆるんでいきます。表面的な緊張だけでなく、構造から整えるため、効果が続きやすいのが特徴です。

Not at all. This is one of the gentlest forms of bodywork there is. There is no cracking, no deep pressure, and no pain at any point. Most people find the session deeply relaxing — many fall asleep. If anything ever feels uncomfortable, just say so and I will adjust immediately.

まったく痛みはありません。骨をポキポキしたり、強く押したりすることは一切ありません。多くの方がうとうとされるほど、深くリラックスできる施術です。もし少しでも気になることがあれば、すぐにお伝えください。

You stay fully clothed throughout the entire session. No undressing, no oils, no special preparation needed. Loose, comfortable clothing is ideal — think yoga wear or anything you can move freely in.

施術は着衣のまま行います。着替えは不要です。オイルも使いません。ヨガウェアのような動きやすい服装が理想ですが、普段着でも問題ありません。

Many people notice a difference after the very first session. How often you come after that depends entirely on you — your body, your lifestyle, and what you're hoping to maintain. Some people come monthly for general upkeep. Others come more frequently at first, then less often as their body settles. There's no fixed programme — I'll give you my honest recommendation after your first session.

多くの方が初回から変化を感じていただけます。その後の頻度はお体の状態や目的によって異なります。月1回のメンテナンスとして通われる方もいれば、最初は集中して来られる方も。初回の後に、正直なアドバイスをお伝えします。

Absolutely safe. Sessions for children are adapted to be even gentler than for adults. I work with children up to age 12. Many families come together — parent and child in the same session — which children often find reassuring. If you have any specific concerns about your child, please feel free to ask before booking.

お子様にも安全にお受けいただけます。大人よりもさらにやさしいアプローチで行います。12歳以下のお子様を対象としており、親子での同時施術も人気です。ご不安な点はご予約前にお気軽にご相談ください。
